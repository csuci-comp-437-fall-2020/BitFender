VirusBox is a rushing-combat type enemy very violent in nature. It is a fearless enemy that's rushing towards and attacking no matter its opponents. The enemy has a unique vicinity detector maintaining its antenna active during the searching for the player within the area(s). If detected close by, its bodily belt will switch its color from white to red indicates if the player is present. The VirusBox's belt is an easy indicator for the player to observe. If they're not within close vicinity, make them an easy target at long-range attacks.
